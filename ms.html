Welcome to
The Snake Game

Objective: Score as many points as possible!!

Rules:

  • Use the arrow keys to navigate the snake.
  • The snake can neither stop nor move backwards.
  • Eating the food increases the length of the snake and adds to your score.
  • The game ends when the snake collides with the wall or itself.
